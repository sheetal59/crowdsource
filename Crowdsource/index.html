ABOUT GOOGLE CROWDSOURCE

WHAT IS GOOGLE CROWDSOURCE?

Ever imagined how Google knows about everything? How are its results so accurate? Actually, every search that we make, everything that we do helps Google work better. Crowdsource android and web app allow the user to answer questions in a gamified UI and help generate training data for ML.

HOW TO GOOGLE CROWDSOURCE?

Every picture we share, every review we provide helps Google train its databases. What these databases are? We all know the term ‘Machine Learning’, it is a way to train a machine using algorithms to do a specific task. The world of technology revolves around data, more data, better training.

WHY GOOGLE CROWDSOURCE?

Artificial Intelligence powers some of the most magical experiences in your favourite apps and products. It helps organize your photos, understand your voice commands, show you the most relevant reviews for products and businesses, and much more! AI learns these skills by studying vast numbers of examples.

ABOUT GOOGLE CROWDSOURCE @VIT BHOPAL

The core team at VIT Bhopal comprises of an eminent mentor and a devoted group of six students who endeavour to grow as a community by learning and educating folks all across the globe in the field of Google Crowdsource. Our main motive is to spread the word about the crowdsource culture by organising various informative and interactive events, webinars and competitions.
